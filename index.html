Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our renewable energy services.

A typical residential solar panel installation takes 2-5 days depending on the system size and roof complexity. Commercial installations may take 1-3 weeks. Our team ensures minimal disruption to your daily routine during the installation process.
On-grid systems connect directly to the electricity grid, allowing you to export excess power. Hybrid systems include battery storage for backup power during outages while still being connected to the grid. We help you choose the best option based on your needs and budget.
We recommend professional maintenance every 6-12 months, including panel cleaning, inverter checks, wiring inspection, and performance monitoring. Regular maintenance can improve system efficiency by up to 25% and extend the lifespan of your solar system.
Yes! We install EV chargers for homes, apartments, offices, and commercial spaces. Our team handles everything from site assessment and electrical evaluation to installation and commissioning. We support all major EV charger brands and types.
Our energy audit includes analysis of current power usage, identification of energy losses, evaluation of equipment efficiency, assessment of power consumption patterns, and personalized recommendations for cost-saving renewable energy solutions.
